Power Module Sends high voltage to Chiller and Heater and sends low voltage to low voltage components.
Heater Receives high voltage from the Power Module.
Chiller Receives both high and low voltage from the Power Module.
Base Unit Control Board Sends low voltage power to components in plumbing shelf and flavor pumps.
Door Board Sends 24VDC low-voltage power to manifold 3, LED and ADA boards and sends 12VDC low-voltage power to SBC.
SBC Also known as the brain of the system, it runs Android OS, connects to internet, and runs the Bevi Dispense App. It receives low-voltage power from DCB.
SBC As the brain of the Standup 2.0 all of the data is received and assessed by the SBC.
